BETTER SOAP -- BETTER SKIN
A product is only as good as the method and the ingredients used to make it. Krasiva Soaps products are hand-made using only 100% natural ingredients. We make our soaps in the old world tradition with pure essential oils, wild and organic herbs, and certified organic vegetable oils, without any harmful chemicals and additives. Our soap-making method is gentle, cruelty-free, ethical, and 100% environmental-friendly. Our soaps are pure, lovely, fragrant, healthy for you and your family, and safe for the environment. None of our Soaps products or ingredients are tested on animals and they are free from additives which have been reported to be harmful to our bodies or the environment, such as Parabens, SLS, Propylene Glycol, petrochemicals, etc. In fact, there is nothing in them but pure essential oils, and only natural colors, if any.
